Meet the Alumni Council: Champions of Connection and Unity

Our Alumni Council is a dynamic team of passionate individuals dedicated to bridging the gap between the past and the present. Comprising both esteemed members of our school staff, students and handpicked alumni leaders, the council is at the heart of fostering vibrant connections within our alumni community.

Each member brings a unique perspective and a wealth of experience to the table, ensuring a diverse range of voices that resonate with every alumnus. Together, we craft meaningful initiatives, organize events, and cultivate an environment where APS Agra's legacy continues to flourish.
With a shared commitment to nurturing relationships and nurturing growth, the Alumni Council stands as a bridge that spans across generations. Join us in celebrating the spirit of camaraderie, as we create lasting connections that enrich the lives of each and every member of the APS family.
Explore the faces behind the council and embark on a journey that echoes with the pride of our shared history and the promise of a bright future.

Mission and Vision

Mission: Our mission at the Army Public School Agra Alumni Council is to strengthen the bond between alumni and the school by fostering a vibrant and inclusive community. We are committed to providing a platform where past and present APSites can connect, collaborate, and contribute to each other's growth.

Vision: Our vision is to create a dynamic Alumni Community that serves as a lifelong network of support and inspiration. We aim to facilitate meaningful interactions, mentorship opportunities, and a sense of belonging that extends beyond graduation. Through our initiatives, events, and collective efforts, we envision APS Agra alumni as ambassadors of positive change in their respective fields, united by shared values and a deep-rooted pride in their alma mater.

At the heart of our mission and vision lies the unwavering commitment to uphold the legacy of Army Public School Agra and empower our alumni to make a positive impact on the world.
